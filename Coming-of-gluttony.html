Reviews of stories

The Second Coming of Gluttony

by Ro Yu Jin (로유진)


The protagonist, Seol Jihu, starts out as a lost soul, a weak-willed gambler who has ruined his life and relationships with his own actions. But through a chance encounter with his future self, he is given the opportunity to change his life for the better.

What follows is a journey of redemption and growth as Seol Jihu struggles to overcome his past and become a better person. The author does a fantastic job of portraying the protagonist's slow, yet steady growth, and the amount of hard work he puts in to become a leader is truly inspiring. The battles in the novel are also well-written, showcasing the desperate nature of the war against the invaders.

In addition to the action and battles, there is also a touch of romance in the story. The relationships between the heroines and the protagonist are well-developed, and their personalities are fully fleshed out. The protagonist's relationships with his family members, which he has damaged with his gambling addiction, are also heart-wrenching and will tug at the reader's emotions.

The world building in The Second Coming of Gluttony is also impressive, with intricate and creative details that make the alternate world feel fully realized. The progression of skills and levels is more difficult than in many other fantasy novels, and the statistics and abilities of the characters are well thought out and balanced.

The characters in the story are also well-developed, with fully fleshed out personalities and motivations. The protagonist's growth and development are particularly noteworthy, as he grapples with his flaws and phobias in a realistic and relatable way. The supporting cast is also well-written, with interesting and complex personalities.

Overall, The Second Coming of Gluttony is a fantastic read for fans of character-driven fantasy stories. The well-developed world, engaging characters, and captivating story will keep readers hooked from start to finish.
