The beginning of my interest

Books have always been an integral part of my life. My siblings encouraged me to learn, and reading was the best way to do it. I started with children's encyclopedias and moved on to science fiction and fantasy novels. I was enthralled by the sheer breadth of knowledge available to me and it expanded my imagination. Reading was like having a second life full of thrilling adventures. I would spend hours lost in my favorite books, picturing myself as the hero and living out exciting escapades.

Reading has been a lifelong passion of mine and has brought me immense joy. It has enabled me to escape into magical realms and has taught me about the world we inhabit. From science to literature, reading has given me a profound understanding of the world and the people in it.

But most importantly, reading has helped me cultivate my own thoughts and ideas. It has allowed me to view things from different angles, to think analytically, and to form my own opinions. Without reading, I don't think I would be the person I am today.
